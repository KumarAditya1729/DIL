# Rhythm & Soul SaaS: Product Roadmap & Vision

## Phase 1: Foundational CRM & ERP (Completed)
- ✅ **Core Student Management**: Admissions, Profiles, Medical Notes.
- ✅ **Batch & Attendance Tracking**: QR Code Kiosk Mode, Instructor scheduling.
- ✅ **Billing Engine**: Automated fee tracking, PDF receipt generation, WhatsApp notification links.
- ✅ **Multi-Tenant Architecture**: Robust PostgreSQL Row Level Security (RLS) isolating academies.

## Phase 2: Advanced Monetization & SaaS Scaling (Completed)
- ✅ **Super Admin Analytics**: Global MRR tracking, tenant directory, activation metrics.
- ✅ **Razorpay Webhooks**: Automated tier upgrades, downgrades, and dunning management.
- ✅ **Onboarding Funnel**: Frictionless 3-step sign-up flow with custom branding injection.

## Phase 3: Immediate Upcoming Releases (Q3 2026)
- 🟡 **Custom Domains**: Fully automated SSL provisioning for academies (e.g., `portal.urban-groove.com`) using Vercel Domains API.
- 🟡 **Advanced Reporting Module**: GST-ready automated monthly financial exports to CSV/Excel.
- 🟡 **Parent Portal App**: A dedicated PWA view for parents to track their child's progress, attendance, and pay fees directly.
- 🟡 **Sentry Integration**: Implementing structured, granular error telemetry to maintain 99.99% uptime.

## Phase 4: Long-term Enterprise Vision (Q4 2026 - Q1 2027)
- 🔴 **AI Insights Engine**: Predictive churn modeling and automated class schedule optimization based on historical attendance data.
- 🔴 **Staff Payroll & HR**: Integrated module for tracking instructor hours, leaves, and automated payout calculation.
- 🔴 **Global Franchising Management**: Allowing enterprise users to manage multiple distinct academy branches under a single master dashboard.
